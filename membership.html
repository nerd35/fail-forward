Fail Forward Membership

Fail Forward membership is open to all tech entrepreneurs all over the world.

How do you become a Fail Forward member?

There are 2 types of membership:

  1. Tech Campus Membership
  2. Tech City Membership

If you are a student tech entrepreneur, you can join a tech campus chapter in your school. If you are a tech entrepreneur with a past or current startup, you can join a tech city chapter in your city.
