Partnership

How can I be a Fail Forward Partner?

If you love what we do at Fail Forward and would love to be a part of us, we welcome you!

We welcome 4 categories of event partners:

Category 1: Platinum partner ($1,000 or #360,000)

If you would like to sponsor our local or global events, have your logo placed on all our communications as a partner, exhibit at our events to increase visibility for your organisation and have access to our local or global database to promote your business, you can sign up as a Platinum partner.

Support at this level entitles the Platinum partner to:

  1. Brand positioning as a startup ecosystem supporter
  2. Recognition of organization as the sole partner of the event
  3. Press coverage brand mentions on Newspapers, online blogs, etc
  4. Display or exhibit products and solutions to stakeholders and potential customers
  5. Placement of organisation name and logo on event flier and banner
  6. Opportunity to distribute appropriate organization promotional materials to all delegates as negotiated.
  7. Opportunity to capture data of participants for further engagement
  8. Opportunity for a senior executive to address participants and speakers at the event.
  9. Logo & link displayed on the Fail Forward Website
  10. Brand awareness: Social media promotion to a new and existing audience
  11. Brand/product promotions to Fail Forward virtual community
Category 2: Gold Partner ($300 or #108,000)

If you would like to sponsor our local or global events, have your logo placed on all our communications as a partner and exhibit at our events to increase visibility for your organisation, you can sign up as a Gold partner.

Support at this level entitles the Gold partner to:

  1. Brand positioning as a startup ecosystem supporter
  2. Press coverage brand mentions on Newspapers, Techpoint, etc
  3. Display or exhibit products and solutions to stakeholders and potential customers
  4. Placement of organisation name and logo on event flier and banner
  5. Opportunity to distribute appropriate organization promotional materials to all delegates as negotiated.
  6. Logo & link displayed on the Fail Forward Website
  7. Brand awareness: Social media promotion to a new and existing audience
Category 3: Silver Partner

This partnership covers all product partnerships ie Venue Partner, Media Partner or Entertainment Partner

Support at this level entitles the Silver partner to:

  1. Brand positioning as a startup ecosystem supporter
  2. Press coverage brand mentions on Newspapers, online blogs, etc
  3. Placement of organisation name and logo on event flier and banner
  4. BOpportunity to distribute appropriate organization promotional materials to all delegates as negotiated.
  5. Logo & link displayed on the Fail Forward Website
  6. Brand awareness: Social media promotion to a new and existing audience

Venue Partners - these can be tech hubs, offices or event halls a Fail Forward local chapter can use for monthly meetings. If you are willing to have us host our events at your space, you are welcome to sign up as a partner.

Media Partners - if you have an online blog, work in radio, tv, or print and can feature our local or global events, you can sign up as a partner.

Entertainment Partners - If you would like to cater to our members during their monthly meet-ups or cater to global events, you are welcome to sign up as a partner.

Category 4: Talent Partner

This partnership provides the talent partner with the opportunity to be a Gold or Platinum Partner at one of our events depending on how many talents acquired from the platform.

Talent partners are organisations or companies who would like access to our talent network where they can hire and collaborate with top tech talents from our rich pool of qualified and experienced candidates.

For more information about recruiting talents from Fail Forward and to sign up as a talent partner, send an email to business@thefailforward.com
