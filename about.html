ABOUT US

Fail Forward started as an idea to bring young entrepreneurs in the tech ecosystem together to learn from the failure stories of renowned, experienced and or popular tech entrepreneurs. This idea was conceived by Tunmise Hassan and Boye Oshinaga, who later brought in Esohe Igbinoba to help execute the first Fail Forward event.

Fail Forward started as a passion for tech entrepreneurs to have access to the right information on what it takes to start, build or grow a startup from those who have walked the road before. The Founders were tired of hearing motivational startup stories and wanted to create a platform where founders came to tell the real truth - how they failed or succeeded and what could be done to get similar results.

At the first Fail Forward event held in March 2019 at Lagos, Nigeria, we had Dr Ola Brown, Founder, Flying Doctors Nigeria, Nubi Kay, Co-Founder, Easy Appetite and Imole Seun, Co-founder, Pass.ng, who poured their hearts and shared with us never-before-heard intimate stories on how they failed, succeeded or evolved into another line of business.

We held the second event in June 2019 at Lagos also, and this time we had Onyeka Akumah, Founder and CEO FarmCrowdy, Femi Iromini, Co-founder & CEO of Turbo and Sheriff Shittu, Founder and CEO, GetDev.co who came loaded with practical insights from their wealth of over 3 decades of collective experience as startup founders.

At the end of the second event, it was clear that there was a need for a more structured community for tech entrepreneurs. It was no longer enough to just hear about how others had failed and succeeded.

We realised we had to help others have access to the right learning, support and network to start, build and grow their startup. Hence, Fail Forward has evolved from just curating failure stories to a global tech community where we incubate tech businesses and help them to succeed globally.

We are a fast-growing community, with members and directors joining us DAILY. If you love what we do and would love to be a part of we are doing, please Join orpartner with us today!
