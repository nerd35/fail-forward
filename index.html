How Does It Work

Fail Forward is a global tech community for entrepreneurs who desire inclusion in the tech ecosystem. It is ideal for those seeking access to funding, business development and the right talents to ideate, build and grow a global profitable startup

At Fail Forward, we provide a platform for startup founders to share their failure-to-success stories and inspire other startup entrepreneurs in the tech ecosystem to learn from their experiences and build successful brands.
1 Fail Forward Local Chapters

Fail Forward operates through physical tech communities in campuses or cities called Fail Forward Chapters. Find out more about how to start a chapter here. Learn more about how to become a member here.

2Fail Forward Stories

We do this through our live quarterly Fail Forward Stories events which hold in every Fail Forward chapter globally, where renowned and experienced tech entrepreneurs come and share their failure stories. We also host monthly live events where members of Fail Forward chapters can come together to share their experiences, learn, collaborate and grow together.

3Forward Cooperative

We help our active members to have access to funding through our Fail Forward Cooperative.

4 Fail Forward Growth Network

To foster community and collaboration, we have an online platform where all student and city members globally can connect called Fail Forward Growth Network.

5Fail Forward Techversity

We also have a learning platform called Techversity, where we curate carefully selected practical lessons from established tech entrepreneurs available for all financial active members.

6Fail Forward Talent Network

To ensure we provide the right level of support to all members, we have a Global Talent Network, called Fail Forward Talent Network where members can create profiles stating their skills, experiences and willingness to collaborate. You can apply as a Talent to join an existing company as a staff or cofounder or as an Employer to hire potential talents into your own company.
